Verify bank details

Pattern principals

  • bank account details are verified via a third party
  • don't soft credit check people, we just need to know if an account number is part of the sort codes data range
  • don't ask for information we don't need
  • give people confidence their transaction in secure
  • allow people to complete the transaction even if they don't have a bank account

Research notes

User research for Get your State Pension found that asking people whether they want to be paid into a bank account or a building society account makes them think about whether they need their pension for everyday spending or for savings.

User research for Get your State Pension found that as long as there are enough indicators on the screen, customers do not mind inputting their bank account details in the service.

User research for Carer’s Allowance found that some people don’t have a bank account. They needed to be able to complete the transaction without providing any bank details.
